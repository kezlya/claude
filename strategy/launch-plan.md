# Launch Plan - From Zero to Channel Validation

**Date:** March 2026
**Validated:** 2026-03-13

**Note:** This plan executes on the APPROVED foundation (ICP targeting + business model hierarchy). The funnel details, messaging, and partner channel remain NEEDS WORK per `strategy/community-growth-strategy.md` and will be revised during execution as validation data comes in.

## Context

Community does not exist yet. Starting from scratch. The goal of this plan is to establish presence (profile + community) and validate the partner referral acquisition channel.

ICP targeting and business model hierarchy are APPROVED - not up for debate. If partner channel fails, we try different channels. The foundation stays.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (This Week)

### Step 1: Register FREE community on Skool
- Register the free community first. Paid tier opens later (Month 2-3).
- Skool allows adding a paid tier to an existing free community.
- A free community with strong branding costs nothing to maintain.
- This unlocks everything else - partner outreach needs a link.

### Step 2: Write Skool profile (About Me + headline)
- This is what community organizers check when Vitaly DMs them.
- Must answer in 3 seconds: "Why should I trust this person with my best members?"
- Can be done in parallel with Step 3.

### Step 3: Write community About page
- This is what a referred member lands on.
- Must answer: "Is this for me, and is this person legit?"
- Use approved messaging from community-growth-strategy.md.
- Can be done in parallel with Step 2.

### Step 4: Seed 3-5 posts in the community
- An empty community looks dead. Minimum content:
  - 1x "Welcome + what this community is about" post
  - 2-3x posts demonstrating production-grade perspective (e.g., "The 5 places your n8n workflow will break in production")
  - 1x credibility/background post
- These also serve as proof of expertise for partner outreach.

## Phase 2: Validate the Channel (Weeks 2-3)

### Step 5: Identify 5 target community organizers
- Find n8n, Make, AI agency communities on Skool with 500-5,000 members.
- Note organizer name, community focus, whether they have a paid tier.
- Prioritize communities where organizer is accessible and has no competing paid tier.

### Step 6: Message 2-3 organizers
- Profile and community are ready to back you up at this point.
- Pitch: "Your best graduates churn because there's nowhere to go. I built the next step."
- Offer: referral fee per member who joins paid tier.
- This is the critical validation test for the entire acquisition strategy.

### Step 7: Offer free audit calls to anyone they refer
- Run 10-15 audit calls total.
- Document every outcome with specific numbers.
- Turn 3 into case studies (anonymized if needed).
- This generates the social proof the Hormozi check flagged as missing.

## Phase 3: Open Paid Tier (Month 2-3)

### Step 8: Launch paid tier when proof + demand exist
- By this point you have: case studies, seed content, partner referrals, people asking for more access.
- Add $250/month paid tier to existing free community on Skool.
- Rewrite paid tier benefits as outcomes with dollar-anchored comparisons (per Hormozi check).

## Dependencies

```
Step 1 (register community)
  |
  +-- Step 2 (profile) --+
  |                       |
  +-- Step 3 (about page)-+-- Step 4 (seed posts) -- Step 5 (find organizers) -- Step 6 (outreach) -- Step 7 (audit calls) -- Step 8 (paid tier)
```
