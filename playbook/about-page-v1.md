# About Page - Version 1 (Original Draft)

Your automation is making money. That part worked.

Now it breaks at 3 AM and you're the only one who can fix it. An enterprise client asked about your security posture and you had nothing to say. Your Make credits are burning through empty polling checks while you sleep. Your n8n instance is running on a single VPS with SQLite and you're afraid to touch it because the last update wiped everything.

The tool that got you here can't get you to 10x from here. That's not a failure - that's a ceiling. And it's a specific, solvable engineering problem.

This community is for founders who have already built something real with AI automation and are hitting that ceiling. Not beginners. Not people exploring whether AI is worth trying. People who have real clients, real revenue, and a stack that works until it doesn't.

I'm Vitaly - Ex-Google, Ex-Microsoft, 20+ years building production systems, currently running a Fractional CTO practice helping technical founders bridge exactly this gap. I'm not teaching you to build your first Zap. I'm here for the conversation you can't have anywhere else: what actually breaks at scale, what security looks like in practice, and what to do when no-code stops being enough.

The community is free and it is selective. Not everyone gets in.

If you apply, the questions are straightforward: tell me what you're running, how you debug it when it breaks, and who's depending on it. There are no wrong answers - but there are answers that show me you have real stakes in this.

If that's you, apply.
